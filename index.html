About Us


Jadavpur University RoboVision Club


The RoboVision Club at Jadavpur University is on a mission to unite students in an educational environment, providing a space to explore robotics, computer vision, and natural language processing through engaging projects. Beyond technical growth, the club focuses on fostering essential interpersonal skills, including teamwork, cooperation, perseverance, and sincerity. Open to students of all experience levels, the club welcomes those without any prior knowledge, emphasizing enthusiasm as the key requirement. Our environment is built on sharing wisdom and experiences among members, catering to individuals who aspire to design circuits and control their bots. Join us at the RoboVision Club, where passion and curiosity meet innovation, and together, we'll explore the exciting possibilities in these cutting-edge fields.
